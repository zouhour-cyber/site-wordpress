== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `service-box` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Service Box menu to customize your Services .
4. Copy your publish Service Box shortcode and then paste into your page or post to display your Service Box
